Single-Assignment C - Installer
===============================

This package install will copy/move the SaC binaries and libraries onto
your system.

Symbolic links to the binaries like `sac2c_p` and `sac4c_p` are located
in /usr/local/bin - please make sure to have this in your $PATH.

For more information, head on over to https://www.sac-home.org/.
